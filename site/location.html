Visitor Information

The show will be located on the second floor of Elings Hall, which is located on the eastern edge of the UCSB campus, behind the east (main) gate entrance and next to Kohn Hall and parking structure 10. (D-6 on the campus map).

UCSB Campus Map

Parking

For Elings Hall: Parking structure 10. Follow the signage for “MAT End of Year Show” for designated parking spaces. Visitors do not have to pay for parking. Please see the parking attendant for a complimentary parking pass.

Lodging

For a list of local lodging options, please visit UCSB’s Local Lodging page.

Travel

UCSB is about 100 miles north of Los Angeles. Visitors can fly directly into Santa Barbara Airport (SBA) and take a shuttle or taxi to the hotel, or fly into Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) and drive up to Santa Barbara – which can take from 1.5 to 2.5 hours, depending on traffic.

Driving directions

From the south, take route 101 north through Santa Barbara, then take route 217, which goes directly to the UCSB campus East gate. From the north, take route 101 south to the Los Carneros Road exit; go south on Los Carneros, then take a left on Mesa Road in the UCSB campus.
